Admission & Application Procedure

The session commences in the month of April for all the classes, except X. Admission to the school is open to all boys and girls between the age of 4 to 16 irrespective of their religion, caste or nationality.


Registration

Registration is open for grades IV – IX for the academic session 2016-17. The registration form along with the school prospectus is available in the school office. Completed registration form along with the Non refundable registration fees should be submitted in favour of Indian Convent School, Kaishoraipatan.


Documents Required

Once the admission is confirmed you have to pay the applicable fees along with the below mentioned documents:

  • Self Attested photocopy of Marksheet/ Report Card
  • Self Attested photocopy of Birth Certificate
  • Original Transfer Certificate
  • Photocopy of Address & Identity proof
  • 5 recent passport size photographs of the child
  • 1 passport size photograph of father and mother
  • 1 passport size photograph of local guardian ( if any)

Final

It is desirable that all admission formalities are completed well before the end of March. However, limited vacancies may exist in the middle of academic year. Information on mid year vacancies may be obtained by e-mailing the school admission office. However, it is best to apply early. All rights reserved with the school management regarding admission.

New Students

  • If a child is withdrawn before the start of the academic year, the admission fee excluding the security and joining kit shall be forfeited in favour of the school.
  • If the child is withdrawn within 15 days joining , three months fee in addition to the admission fee shall be deducted and balance will be refunded.
  • In case of withdrawal the refunds will be made up to 2 months. (Including Security) The security amount when refunded is interest free.

Existing Students

  • If the child is withdrawn in between any Term (irrespective of his/her date of joining), that Term’s fee shall be charged / forfeited in favour of the school.
  • Term dates 1 Term (Ist April – 30th September) 2 Term(Ist October – 31st March)
  • If the child is to be withdrawal in the month of March, a prior 3 Month notice is to be given in writing i.e. latest by 31st December. If the notice is given after the said date, the security money of the child shall be forfeited.
  • School Leaving Certificate (Transfer Certificate) will be issued only after clearance of dues and payment of amount(s) in favour of the school.
  • The withdrawal form in the prescribed format should be filled only by the parent and be submitted to the admission office only. The receiving at school of the original copy of duly filled and signed withdrawal form, will be treated as final date of withdrawal.
